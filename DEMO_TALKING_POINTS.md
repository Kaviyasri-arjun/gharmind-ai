## 5 MAGIC MOMENTS IN THE DEMO

### 1. The 94% Motor Prediction (Minute 1:45)
> The system found 14 consecutive Fridays of motor data, cross-referenced
> with Diwali week context, and produced a confidence score in real time.
> No rule was written. That confidence was computed from evidence.

### 2. Gharji's Hinglish Response (Minute 2:30)
> It didn't translate from English. It understood the household context
> — Diwali, JEE exam, tonight's power cut — and generated a coherent
> family plan in the language Anjali actually thinks in.

### 3. The Cascade Chain (Minute 3:15)
> Watch carefully: power cut → WiFi drops → Rahul's Zoom drops.
> That's three hops of cause-and-effect the simulator traced
> automatically through the household state graph.

### 4. Rahul's Zoom Discovery (Minute 3:25)
> Nobody in the demo family thought of Rahul's Zoom call.
> The AI found it — because it cross-referenced the power cut time
> against the WFH schedule and found the collision.
> That's the moment that wins.

### 5. Household Zoning for Diwali+JEE (Minute 4:10)
> Instead of telling the family to choose between the festival and the exam,
> it divided the house into zones. Quiet side, festive side.
> Both happen. This is what an Operating System does.
